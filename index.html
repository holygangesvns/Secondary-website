Our Story

A love letter to Banaras.

Holy Ganges Guide began the way most beautiful things do — slowly, accidentally, around a clay cup of chai. Three friends, one rooftop, and a city that refused to let us leave. We're not here to sell you a checklist. We're here to slow you down. To introduce you to the boatman whose grandfather knew the river. To the tabla teacher who plays for free on Tuesdays. To the alleyway that smells like jasmine at 4 a.m.
